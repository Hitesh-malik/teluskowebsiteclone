About Telusko

Started with the aim not just to teach but to educate 'Aliens' in Navin's words. We provide industry level quality content on technologies like Java, Blockchain, Python, JavaScript and their implementations.

Telusko is led by Navin Reddy, a Developer, Corporate trainer, and YouTuber with 10+ years of experience in technology. He has worked with various technologies, with Java and Blockchain being his forte.

We have built a community of over 300,000 students worldwide and continue to grow every day. Our mission is to simplify complex technical concepts and make them accessible to everyone.
